## FQDN Policies


```
apiVersion: crd.antrea.io/v1alpha1
kind: ClusterNetworkPolicy
metadata:
  name: acnp-fqdn-all-foobar
spec:
  priority: 1
  appliedTo:
  - podSelector:
      matchLabels:
        app: client
  egress:
  - action: Drop
    to:
      - fqdn: "*foobar.com"
```
